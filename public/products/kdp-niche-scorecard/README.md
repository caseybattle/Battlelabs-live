## Buyer Promise

This product helps a self-publisher, printable seller, Canva template seller, or low-content book creator turn one rough KDP niche idea into a structured go/no-go snapshot. The report focuses on buyer intent, giftability, competition clarity, production simplicity, and evergreen demand so the buyer can decide what to test next before building a full book or listing package.

The scorecard is a planning tool. It does not predict Amazon search rank, sales volume, royalty income, bestseller status, or ad performance.
