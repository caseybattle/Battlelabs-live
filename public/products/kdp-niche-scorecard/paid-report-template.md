Paperback guided journal with 100 to 120 interior pages, section dividers, writing prompts, optional photo/keepsake pages, and a gift-ready cover.

## Cover Direction

Thumbnail goal:

The cover should communicate "gift for Grandma" and "family memories" at thumbnail size without requiring the subtitle to be read.

Visual direction:

- Color mood: warm, giftable, soft, and readable
- Typography: large readable title, simple subtitle, high contrast
- Image or pattern: subtle florals, family-photo frame motif, handwritten note texture, or keepsake box detail
- Avoid: cluttered collage covers, hard-to-read script, copied competitor layouts, and misleading "family tree research" imagery if the interior is only prompts

Cover compliance notes:

- Cover should not use copyrighted characters, brand logos, or copied competitor layouts.
- Cover should accurately reflect the interior format and buyer promise.
- Any AI-generated or Canva-sourced visuals must follow the applicable license terms.

## Launch Checklist

Product build:

- Confirm exact buyer, occasion, and product format.
- Create or revise the full interior based on the scorecard recommendation.
- Export a print-ready PDF and inspect margins, bleed, page order, and blank pages.
- Create a short sample preview for organic testing.
- Save source files in Drive with versioned filenames.

Marketplace readiness:

- Check title, subtitle, author name, and series naming for policy and trademark risk.
- Confirm listing copy matches the actual product.
- Review category, keyword, and metadata choices manually.
- Avoid claims about guaranteed sales, rankings, reviews, therapy outcomes, income, or platform approval.

Organic demand test:

- Publish one static landing page or product preview page.
- Share one free sample link with UTM tracking.
- Post three value-first organic posts without spammy claims.
- Track visits, sample clicks, checkout clicks, buyer questions, and refunds.
- Improve, clone, or stop based on observed behavior.
