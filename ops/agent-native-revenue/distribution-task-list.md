# Organic Distribution Task List - Memory Journal Gift Launch Kit

## Rules

- No paid ads or paid placements.
- No scraped email lists or bulk cold email.
- No fake reviews, fake testimonials, fake scarcity, or income claims.
- Every action needs an artifact: URL, draft, screenshot, metric entry, or source note.

## SEO Foundation

Acceptance criteria: a keyword sheet exists with keyword, intent, platform, title use, and priority.

Tasks:

- Build 30-50 keywords around memory journal gifts, guided family journals, grandparent keepsakes, and KDP printable templates.
- Create title formulas for the landing page, Payhip/Gumroad, Pinterest pins, and later KDP.
- Write a 150-250 word product description that leads with buyer intent and avoids keyword stuffing.
- Create FAQ answers for recipient, format, printable use, religious neutrality, AI disclosure, refund terms, and platform-safe use.

## Marketplace Setup

Acceptance criteria: listing fields can be pasted into a product marketplace with no missing core fields.

Tasks:

- Prepare product title, short description, long description, price, refund terms, and file list.
- Create image captions for the five promo SVGs.
- Prepare four angles: sentimental gift, family-history preservation, parent/grandparent interview, holiday gift.
- Avoid bestseller, ranking, review, or revenue claims.

## Pinterest And Social Assets

Acceptance criteria: every asset has audience, hook, image idea, caption, CTA, and destination placeholder.

Tasks:

- Create 25 pin concepts: 10 gift-guide, 5 questions-to-ask, 5 occasion, 5 product preview.
- Create 8 board names.
- Create 10 short captions.
- Create 5 short-form video scripts.
- Create 5 carousel outlines.
- Use soft CTAs: "See the preview", "Save this gift idea", "Use this question tonight".

## Warm Gmail Drafts

Acceptance criteria: drafts are warm-only and require a real relationship reason.

Rules:

- Max 5 warm emails per day if sending later.
- No attachments unless requested.
- One product link max.
- One follow-up only.
- Stop on no reply, opt-out, or discomfort.

## Community And Partnership Distribution

Acceptance criteria: 30 opportunities with URL, audience, rules, contact method, pitch, and risk level.

Tasks:

- Find gift guide blogs accepting submissions.
- Find family history newsletters.
- Find genealogy communities with promotion rules.
- Find senior-care and family project blogs.
- Find Pinterest group boards if active.
- Draft 10 non-promotional value posts that can stand alone without a product link.

## Metrics

Track:

- Listing impressions.
- Product page visits.
- Free sample downloads.
- Checkout clicks.
- Sales.
- Refunds.
- Pinterest saves.
- Pinterest outbound clicks.
- Email sends and replies.
- Qualitative buyer language.

Scale if:

- 3+ organic sales.
- 10+ saves on multiple pins.
- 2%+ Pinterest outbound click rate.
- Repeated buyer language asks for adjacent variants.

Revise if:

- People click checkout but do not buy.
- People download sample but do not click checkout.
- People reply but ask for a different recipient or format.

Kill if:

- No meaningful clicks, opt-ins, replies, saves, or sales after targeted distribution.
- Product requires custom explanation to sell.
- Refunds or complaints show the value proposition is weak.
