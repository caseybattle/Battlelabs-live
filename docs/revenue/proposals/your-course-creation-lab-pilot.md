# Your Course Creation Lab Pilot Build

## Goal
Sort creator leads by stage and need earlier so the front-end path feels more relevant and easier to follow up on.

## Problem Summary
From the teardown, the main gap appears to be:
- different creator stages are likely being pushed into too-similar a path before qualification happens

This matters because:
- planning, building, launching, and optimizing creators need different next steps
- broader intake makes follow-up less specific
- creators who are interested but not ready for direct contact may leave without entering a useful nurture path

## Pilot Scope
This pilot build focuses on one specific outcome:
- add course-stage segmentation and follow-up before live contact

Typical components:
- CTA cleanup by readiness
- segmented inquiry path by course stage
- short async fit form
- follow-up sequence matched to stage
- async handoff notes

## Deliverables
- segmented front-end inquiry path
- course-stage qualification form
- follow-up sequence for stage-specific leads

## Timeline
- target turnaround: 72 hours to 5 days
- delivery model: async only
