# Sarah Raanan Pilot Build

## Goal
Tighten the front-end lead path for business coaching and content strategy so warm visitors are segmented earlier and followed up without relying entirely on live booking.

## Problem Summary
From the teardown, the main gap appears to be:
- both offers push toward live discovery calls before the visitor gets an easier async qualification step

This matters because:
- business coaching and content strategy likely attract different buyer intent
- warmer leads may leave before choosing a live booking path
- Sarah loses information that could make follow-up more relevant and easier to review asynchronously

## Pilot Scope
This pilot build focuses on one specific outcome:
- add a segmented async qualification layer before the live call step

Typical components:
- CTA cleanup on the booking page
- one split inquiry path for coaching vs content strategy
- one short qualification form
- one follow-up sequence aligned to the selected path
- async handoff notes

## Deliverables
- segmented inquiry entry point for the two service paths
- shorter async form and routing logic
- follow-up email sequence for each selected offer

## Timeline
- target turnaround: 72 hours to 5 days
- delivery model: async only

## Price
- pilot build fee: `$350`

## What is not included
- full website rebuild
- full copy overhaul across the entire site
- CRM migration or complex backend system setup
- ongoing retainer support unless added separately

## Why this scope
The pilot is narrow on purpose. It fixes the front-end leak first so Sarah can see whether segmentation and follow-up improve lead quality before expanding the system.
