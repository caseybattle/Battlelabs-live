# Jonathan Dunnett Pilot Build

## Goal
Create a softer async first step for speaking and consulting leads so interested prospects can enter the pipeline before committing to a live booking.

## Problem Summary
From the teardown, the main gap appears to be:
- the current path moves interested visitors too quickly into live booking without a lower-friction async entry point

This matters because:
- warmer leads may not be ready to commit to a call immediately
- speaking and consulting inquiries often need a small amount of qualification before a live conversation
- Jonathan is likely missing interest from people who would respond to a lighter first step

## Pilot Scope
This pilot build focuses on one specific outcome:
- add a pre-call async capture and follow-up path for speaking and consulting inquiries

Typical components:
- new softer CTA before the booking step
- short inquiry form tied to speaking vs consulting needs
- follow-up sequence for qualified leads
- async handoff and sorting logic

## Deliverables
- async inquiry entry point before the call booking step
- segmented form for speaking vs consulting
- short follow-up sequence to keep warm leads moving

## Timeline
- target turnaround: 72 hours to 5 days
- delivery model: async only

## Price
- pilot build fee: `$350`

## What is not included
- full brand redesign
- deep speaker funnel build across multiple pages
- large automation stack beyond the scoped inquiry flow
- ongoing support unless added separately

## Why this scope
The pilot proves whether a softer entry point captures warm interest more effectively than pushing every visitor directly to live booking.

## Close
If this scope looks right, the next step is a payment link and a simple async kickoff.
