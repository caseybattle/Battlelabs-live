# Pilot Build FAQ

## What is a Battle Labs pilot build?
A focused async project designed to fix one front-end bottleneck first:
- lead capture
- inquiry routing
- follow-up
- website clarity
- checkout or handoff friction

It is intentionally narrow so the work can ship quickly and prove value before scope expands.

## How much does it cost?
Most first-fit pilot builds start in the `$250-$500` range.

The exact price depends on:
- how many moving parts are being changed
- whether the issue is page-level, form-level, or flow-level
- how much follow-up logic needs to be written or connected

## How long does it take?
Typical turnaround:
- `72 hours` to `5 days`

That timeline assumes:
- the scope is narrow
- required access or assets are provided quickly
- the project does not turn into a full rebuild

## Do we need a call?
No.

Battle Labs is designed to work async:
- forms
- email
- Loom
- scoped proposal
- payment link
- kickoff notes

## What is not included?
- full rebrand
- full website rebuild unless separately scoped
- custom app development
- large CRM migrations
- open-ended consulting

## What happens after the pilot?
If the pilot fixes the right bottleneck, the next step can be:
- another focused build
- a larger system scope
- a support retainer

If the pilot reveals a different problem than expected, the next phase gets scoped from that reality instead of guessing upfront.

## Why not just do the whole project at once?
Because the fastest way to real proof is to fix one important leak first.

That keeps:
- the price lower
- the timeline shorter
- the sales process easier
- the delivery cleaner
