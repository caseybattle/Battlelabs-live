# Teardown Delivery Playbook

## Objective
Respond to interested prospects with a fast, useful teardown that proves Battle Labs can diagnose and simplify their front-end sales system.

## Delivery format
Use one of these:
- short email
- Loom under 5 minutes
- short PDF or text breakdown only if asked

## Structure
1. What is working
2. Where the likely conversion leak is
3. One change to make first
4. What Battle Labs would build
5. Soft close

## Rules
- stay specific to the public page
- do not fabricate metrics or outcomes
- avoid calling the whole business broken
- point to one fix, not ten
- make the next step feel small and async

## Pilot build framing
Use language like:

> I would treat this as a focused pilot build: tighten the front-end flow, route the inquiry cleanly, and set up the follow-up sequence so the system is easier to run.

## Success sign
A good teardown should make the prospect say one of these:
- "That is exactly the issue."
- "Can you show me what that build would include?"
- "How much would a pilot like that cost?"
