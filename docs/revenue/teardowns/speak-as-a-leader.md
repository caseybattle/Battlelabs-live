# Async Teardown: Speak as a Leader

## What is working
- The speaking and coaching positioning is clear.
- The page likely attracts motivated buyers with an obvious need.
- The live consultation path is visible enough for strong-intent traffic.

## Likely conversion leak
- The current path appears to route visitors straight into a consultation call.
- That usually loses warmer leads who need a lower-friction async first step before they commit to live scheduling.
- Speaking and coaching leads may also need light qualification before the same next step makes sense for both.

## One change to make first
Insert a lighter async inquiry layer before the consultation:
- one short fit form
- one route for speaking-related leads and one for coaching-related leads
- one short follow-up path for people not ready to book immediately

## What Battle Labs would build
- softer first CTA before the consultation
- segmented inquiry by speaking vs coaching intent
- short async follow-up sequence
- simpler handoff for qualified leads

## Tight response version
I would start by making the front-end path lighter before the consultation call becomes the default next step. A short async inquiry layer plus segmented follow-up would likely recover warmer leads who are interested but not ready to book live. If you want, I can scope that into a pilot build and keep the whole process async.
