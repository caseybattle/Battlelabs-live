# Segment Review Rubric

## Purpose
Decide whether coaches or creators deserve the next block of attention based on evidence, not preference.

## Score each segment from 0-2

### Reply quality
- `0`: no real replies
- `1`: replies are polite but soft
- `2`: replies show clear commercial interest

### Problem clarity
- `0`: prospects seem confused
- `1`: some understand the issue
- `2`: prospects immediately recognize the bottleneck

### Offer fit
- `0`: pilot offer feels mismatched
- `1`: partial fit
- `2`: pilot offer feels natural and easy to explain

### Proof potential
- `0`: hard to turn into usable proof
- `1`: some proof potential
- `2`: clear before/after or case-study potential

### Closing potential
- `0`: no real path toward proposal
- `1`: some teardown interest
- `2`: clear path toward teardown, proposal, and close

## Decision rule
- `8-10`: keep feeding the segment
- `5-7`: keep testing but tighten the message
- `0-4`: pause the segment and shift volume elsewhere
