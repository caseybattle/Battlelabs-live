# Daily Runbook

## Daily Targets
- 30 to 50 targeted outreach touches
- 5 to 10 new qualified leads added to the tracker
- 1 teardown-style asset drafted or sent
- 1 proof-oriented post or short content asset advanced
- tracker reviewed before ending the session

## Daily Sequence
1. Review tracker and follow-up overdue leads
2. Source new leads for the active niche
3. Send first-touch outreach
4. Send follow-ups
5. Draft one teardown or proof asset
6. Capture objections and repeated pains
7. Log data and decide whether to keep, adjust, or kill the current angle

## Weekly Review
- reply rate
- positive reply rate
- qualified lead count
- proposals sent
- wins
- time to deliver
- repeated pains worth productizing
