# Niche 01: Coaches and Consultants Already Selling Online

## Why this niche is first
- strong overlap with the current Battle Labs proof and service language
- common pattern: call-first sales flow with weak async capture
- easy to diagnose from public pages without deep research
- easy to pitch a focused fix:
  lead capture, follow-up, form routing, and lighter async qualification

## Repeated patterns from the first seed list
- call-first discovery or compatibility flow
- broad “work with me” pages trying to serve multiple offers at once
- little or no async starter offer
- weak segmentation between offer types
- likely lead loss from visitors who are interested but not ready to book live

## Battle Labs angle for this niche
Use this promise most often:

> I help coaches and consultants tighten lead capture, follow-up, and inquiry routing so warm prospects do not stall before the sale.

## Best first-touch hooks
- "I noticed your page asks for a live call before giving people an easier async next step."
- "You have interest and authority already; the gap looks like what happens between the page visit and the follow-up."
- "I think one focused system change could make your inquiry flow cleaner without adding a heavy setup."
