# Battle Labs Offer Sheet

## Core Offer
Battle Labs builds solo-led lead capture, follow-up, website, and simple automation systems for founders, creators, coaches, consultants, and small service businesses.

## Best-Fit Problems
- too many inquiries fall through
- no follow-up after someone fills out a form
- website looks fine but does not convert
- intake is messy across forms, email, Stripe, or DMs
- content and admin work eat up time that should go into selling

## Primary Offer
**Lead Capture + Follow-Up System**

What it can include:
- landing page or hero/funnel rewrite
- cleaner inquiry form and routing
- follow-up email sequence
- simple automation layer
- async handoff notes or Loom

## Fallback Offer
**Website Rebuild + Inquiry Flow**

What it can include:
- website refresh or focused rebuild
- offer clarity and CTA cleanup
- inquiry form routing
- Stripe, Gumroad, or checkout connection
- simple chat or onboarding layer if useful

## Pricing Ladder
1. Pilot build: `$250-$500`
2. Core build: `$1,000-$1,500`
3. Premium build: `$2,000-$3,000`
4. Support retainer: `$150-$500/mo`

## Sales Rules
- no calls
- async only
- email, forms, Loom, DMs, and checkout links only

## Promise Language
Use this sentence most often:

> I build lead capture, follow-up, website, and simple automation systems for businesses that need cleaner operations and more qualified inquiries.

## Offer Boundaries
Do not lead with:
- generic “AI agents”
- broad agency wording
- fake ROI or fake case study claims

Lead with:
- lead capture
- follow-up
- inquiry flow
- websites that convert
- simple automations that reduce manual work
