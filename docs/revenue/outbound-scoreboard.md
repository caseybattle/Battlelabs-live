## Success thresholds
- `3%+` positive reply rate keeps the segment live
- at least `1` teardown request per `20` touches is a healthy early signal
- at least `1` pilot proposal per `40` touches shows real commercial movement

## Kill or adjust thresholds
- if a segment gets `0` positive replies after `20` touches, tighten the promise or pause the segment
- if replies are curious but non-commercial, narrow the offer from broad automation to lead capture + follow-up
- if creator language underperforms coach language, shift volume back toward coaches until the copy is stronger
