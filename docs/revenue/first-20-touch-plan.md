# First 20 Touch Plan

## Objective
Turn the first seed niche into a real outbound cycle with enough touches to learn quickly without drifting into random outreach.

## Touch Allocation
- 8 first-touch emails to the seed list
- 8 first-touch DMs or alternate channel touches where available
- 4 follow-up slots reserved for the strongest prospects after 48 hours

## Sequence
1. Send the 8 personalized emails from `outreach-personalized-coaches.md`
2. Log each send in the tracker with `status=contacted`
3. Mark any soft interest, bounce, or no-response in the notes
4. After 48 hours, send the short follow-up to the 4 strongest-fit leads
5. Review reply quality before sourcing the next 12 leads

## Logging Rules
- update `last_touch_date` on every send
- if a lead responds with curiosity but no immediate project, move to `replied`
- if a lead asks for details or teardown, move to `qualified`
- if a lead is clearly wrong-fit, mark `lost` and write the reason in `notes`

## Success Signals
- at least 1 request for the teardown
- at least 1 reply showing commercial intent
- clearer language about which pain point gets the strongest response

## Failure Signals
- no replies at all
- replies are polite but non-commercial
- the same confusion shows up across multiple prospects

## Next Action If Weak
- tighten the promise from broad automation to one narrower outcome:
  `lead capture + follow-up for coaches and consultants`
