# Microproduct Experiment 01: Reply Deck

## Type
Small downloadable template/system

## Brand
Use a separate micro-brand: **Reply Deck**

## Buyer
Coaches, consultants, creators, and small service businesses that already get inquiries but do not follow up well.

## Problem
Warm leads come in, but the business does not have a clean follow-up sequence, clear inquiry routing, or a fast way to reply consistently.

## Offer
A lightweight follow-up starter system:
- inquiry flow audit checklist
- 5-message async follow-up sequence
- form field template
- qualification rubric
- setup instructions for a simple lead pipeline

## CTA
Buy the starter kit or join the waitlist for the expanded version.

## Initial Price
`$19-$39`

## Channel
- Battle Labs outreach follow-up
- X and Instagram proof posts
- email list mentions after service relevance is proven

## Success Threshold
Keep the experiment if at least one of these happens within 14 days of launch:
- 10+ waitlist signups from targeted traffic
- 3+ purchases
- repeated replies asking for access or a fuller version

## Kill Threshold
Kill or reposition if:
- messaging is still unclear after 2 iterations
- targeted traffic arrives but signups stay near zero
- maintaining the experiment steals time from the service engine without buyer pull

## Why This Is First
- aligned with the Battle Labs primary offer
- cheap to package
- easy to explain in under 10 seconds
- can be sold async
- can later expand into a higher-ticket build, audit, or software-like tool

## Launch Requirements
- short landing page
- checkout link
- one proof-oriented post
- one outreach mention for relevant prospects
- tracking for visits, clicks, signups, and purchases
