# Lead Qualification and Pipeline

## Pipeline Statuses
- `new`
- `contacted`
- `replied`
- `qualified`
- `proposal sent`
- `won`
- `lost`

## Qualification Rubric
Score each lead from `0-2` on each category.

### Offer Fit
- `0`: no clear monetized offer
- `1`: offer exists but conversion path is weak
- `2`: offer exists and improving inquiry flow would likely matter

### Speed to Value
- `0`: would require major custom development
- `1`: moderate complexity
- `2`: can be improved with a focused site + form + follow-up build

### Async Fit
- `0`: clearly wants meetings or agency-style management
- `1`: unclear
- `2`: can buy and operate async

### Proof Fit
- `0`: hard to explain or show later
- `1`: some proof potential
- `2`: clean before/after proof opportunity

## Decision Rule
- `7-8`: high priority
- `5-6`: medium priority
- `0-4`: low priority or skip

## Kill Rules
- fewer than 3 positive replies per 200 touches: change niche or message
- zero serious buyer intent after 300 touches: replace the current offer framing
- delivery taking more than 6 hours for the entry offer: narrow scope before selling more
