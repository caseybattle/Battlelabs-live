# KDP Cover Direction Brief Workbook

SKU: `KDP-COVER-001`

This workbook helps you write a cover direction brief you can hand to a designer (or use to create an original cover yourself).

This is a planning aid. No sales or ranking results are guaranteed.

## Quick start (5 minutes)

1. Fill the one-sentence buyer + promise.
2. Choose 5 category cue words (without copying).
3. Set one contrast rule for thumbnail readability.
4. Decide imagery direction (type-only vs symbol vs illustration vs photo-based).
5. Run the thumbnail checklist.

## Worksheet F — Thumbnail checklist

- Title is readable at small size.
- Category is obvious at a glance.
- Buyer/recipient signal is present.
- No trademarked phrases without permission.
- No copied layouts from competitor covers.

## Compliance reminders

- No ranking, income, or sales outcomes are guaranteed.
- Do not copy competitor covers or listings.
- Check trademarks, category rules, metadata rules, and platform policies before publishing.
- If AI assisted the cover or interior content, follow the required AI-content disclosure rules for the platform (including KDP).

## Fictional example brief (for structure only)

Example product: “Mindful Gardening Log” (fictional)

Buyer: beginner gardeners who want a calm weekly habit  
Promise: track plants, watering, and seasonal notes  
Cue words: “calm”, “natural”, “simple grid”, “botanical”, “minimal”  
Color constraints: greens + off-white, high contrast title  
Typography: clean sans serif, strong title hierarchy  
Imagery: single leaf symbol, no photos  

This example is fictional. Do not derive your cover from a real competitor cover.
