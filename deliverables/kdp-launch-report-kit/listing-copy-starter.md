## Listing Description Starter

Give Grandma a simple way to write down the stories, memories, and little family details that are easy to lose over time.

This guided memory journal is designed as a thoughtful gift for grandmothers and the families who want to preserve their history. The prompts help her record childhood memories, family traditions, favorite recipes, life lessons, meaningful moments, and messages for future generations.

Inside, the journal can include sections for:

- Childhood and family roots prompts
- Favorite memories and traditions
- Recipes, holidays, and family gatherings
- Life lessons and advice
- Photo, keepsake, and message pages

Use it as a quiet, meaningful gift that helps Grandma share her story in her own words.
